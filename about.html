Our Story

From Vision to Impact

Code Beacons Technologies was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between complex technology and real business value. Based in Pune, India, we started as a small team of passionate technologists who believed that the right technology, implemented the right way, can transform any business.

Over the years, we've grown into a full-service IT consulting firm, serving clients across industries including healthcare, finance, retail, and manufacturing. Our approach combines deep technical expertise with strategic business thinking, ensuring that every solution we deliver creates measurable impact.

Today, we continue to push boundaries, embracing emerging technologies like AI, cloud computing, and cybersecurity to help our clients stay ahead of the curve. Our name reflects our purpose — to be the beacon that guides businesses through the ever-evolving technology landscape.
